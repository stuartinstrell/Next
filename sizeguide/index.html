How To Measure

To choose the correct size for you, measure your body as follows:

1Top of Shoulder

Dress lengths are given at a worn length and are taken by measuring from the top of the collarbone, to the hem or the longest point

2Bust

Take measurements around fullest part

3Waist

Measure around the natural waistline. (find the natural crease by bending to one side

4Hips

Feet together, measure the fullest part

5Inside Leg

Run the tape measure from the top of the inside leg at the crotch to the ankle bone
