The benefits of your Next Account in store

GET YOURS NOW

The Next Directory Card makes shopping in store easier than ever!

Visit any one of our 500 stores in the UK and your Directory Card will link your purchases straight to your Directory Account*. Collect and return items in store using your Directory Card as identification.

* The Next Directory Card allows you to use your Next Credit Account to purchase, return and exchange goods in all Next Group stores. Purchases will be added to your credit account normally within 1 day of purchase, and will appear on both your next monthly statement and recent activity in “My Account”. To be eligible you must be an existing Next Credit Account holder and have received at least 3 account statements and must have a good account payment history.

We reserve the right to refuse a request for a Next Directory Card, please refer to the Next Terms & Conditions for more details.
